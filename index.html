Bio

Shrawan received his medical degree from Imperial College, London (ranked 2nd globally by QS 2015) with a distinction in General Practice. Thereafter he practiced both general medicine and general surgery in London for a number of years.

He has recently moved to New York to seek new opportunities focusing on the business and management end of healthcare.

Outside of Medicine his past positions include: Global Advisor for a US health benefits consulting firm, Executive Board member for an $11M charity, Corporate Strategy Intern at one of the world’s largest healthcare insurers, and project lead for a risk management assignment for the world’s second largest financial clearing house. He also has also been heavily involved in the London start-up scene focusing on health and education technologies.
