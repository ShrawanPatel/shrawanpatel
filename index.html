A Bit About Me

I'm a physician by training, but an inventor at heart. Having spent 7+ years in healthcare working as a clinician, implementing IT systems, creating new service lines, working with large datasets, and building businesses I now find myself using everything I've learned at the intersection of health and technology.

Speaking

I love to discuss innovation in health technology and its application in a real world setting to improve the lives of patients. What works, what doesn't work, and how we continue to take strides foward. Feel free to reach out to me if I might be a good fit for your event.
